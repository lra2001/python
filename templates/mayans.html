Mayan Civilization Highlights

The Maya left behind majestic stone cities, astronomical observatories, and complex writings that still captivate archaeologists.

  • Architectural feats: Temples and pyramids at Chichén Itzá, Tikal, Palenque.
  • Advanced cosmology: Intricate understanding of astronomy and precise calendars.
  • Written legacy: Glyphs and codices recording history, rituals, and governance.
  • Top 10 facts: From chocolate origins to zero concept, their achievements were vast.
