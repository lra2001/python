Egypt - From Ancient Marvels to Modern Wonders

Journey through ancient Egypt and explore the majestic Pyramids of Giza, the enigmatic Sphinx, and the grandeur of pharaohs.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Cairo
  • Era: Ancient Egypt thrived over 5,000 years ago
  • Geography: Centered around the Nile Valley and Delta

Ancient Egypt

Ancient Egyptians built iconic monuments such as pyramids, temples, and kept elaborate recordsâ€”over 30 miles of ancient papyri reveal details about logistics during pyramid construction.

  • Pyramids of Giza: Built during the Old Kingdom; About 118-138 pyramids include the famous Great Pyramid and the Sphinx.
  • Construction techniques: Blocks (~2.5 tons) moved on sleds over wet sand to reduce friction.
  • Saqqara Step Pyramid: The earliest pyramid (~2630 BC), designed by Imhotep.

Top Things to Do Today

  • Pyramids & Sphinx: Wander through the ancient Giza plateau.
  • Egyptian Museum (Cairo): Home to Tutankhamun's treasures and royal mummies.
  • Valley of the Kings (Luxor): See decorated royal tombs, including King Tut's.
  • Khan el-Khalili Bazaar: Bustling market for spices, crafts & souvenirs.
  • Mummification Museum (Luxor): Displays animal mummies and embalming techniques.

Fun Facts:

  • The Great Pyramid of Giza is over 4,500 years old.
  • The Sphinx is carved from a single limestone ridge.
  • Egypt's ancient civilization invented papyrus and hieroglyphics.
